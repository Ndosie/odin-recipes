Rice

One of the most cooked dish in Sub-Saharan African countries

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 table spoons of salt
  • 2 table spoons of oil

Steps

  1. Add water to the rice cooker
  2. Wash rice and add to the rice cooker
  3. Add salt and oil and mix
  4. Set the rice cooker to cook for 10 minutes
  5. After the ten minutes the dish is done
