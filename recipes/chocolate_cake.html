Chocolate Cake

A three layer chocolate cake with rasberry toppings

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of wheat flour
  • 2 table spoons of Cocoa
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 2 eggs
  • 20 rasberries
  • 1 cup of milk

Steps

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients(flour, sugar) into one bowl
  2. Mix the fluid ingredients(eggs, milk) into one bowl
  3. Slowly pour the mixture of fluid ingredients into dry ingredients while mixing
  4. After it is fully mixed pour it into baking tray and bake for 45 minutes at 450F
  5. While baking prepare the toppings by melting the chocolate chips
  6. After the cake is done poor the melted chocolate on top of it
  7. Decorate with rasberries
